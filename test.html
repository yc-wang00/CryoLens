Mechanism of Action

Betaine acts as a preferential excluder, increasing the surface tension of water around proteins, while Trehalose replaces water molecules in the hydration shell. At 4°C, this prevents formamide from infiltrating the hydrophobic core of sensitive enzymes, effectively halting denaturation.
